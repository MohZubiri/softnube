Why this system?

Designed to fit how customs clearance offices actually work

A clearance office needs more than just managing "shipments" — it has to track legal deadlines, the status of the customs declaration, containers, trucks and local permits, with alerts before time runs out. This system was built around exactly that reality.

  • All transport types in one place
    Sea by containers, land by trucks, and local vehicle clearance — instead of scattered systems.
  • Alerts before the deadline
    The system flags shipments whose remaining days are running low and surfaces them on the dashboard.
  • Customs declaration linked to the shipment
    Record customs data, the customs port and its status, tied directly to each shipment.
  • Reports ready for clients and management
    Instant export to PDF or Excel by company, department or shipping type.

FAQ

Common questions about how the system works

Does the system support different shipping types?+
Yes. The system manages three independent tracks: sea freight (by container and shipping lines), land freight (by trucks and trailers with tracking), and local vehicle clearance with permit issuance — all shown together in a single dashboard.
How does shipment-stage tracking work?+
Each shipment has 9 stages from "Manufacturing" to "Warehouse arrival" (factory departure, source port, in transit, port arrival, customs, released, transport…). Each stage records its date and owner, and a complete tracking history is kept that you can return to at any time.
How does the system warn me before the deadline?+
The system calculates the remaining days for each shipment, and when the deadline gets close (for example within 3 days) the shipment appears in the alerts list on the dashboard, sorted from the nearest to expire — so you never miss a legal deadline.
Can customs declarations be recorded and linked to shipments?+
Yes. There is a dedicated customs-data module that lets you record the customs declaration, the customs port and its status, and link it to the relevant shipment, so everything about the shipment is in one place.
What reports are available and how do I export them?+
The system provides reports for sea, land and local clearance, plus summaries by company and department. Any report can be exported instantly as PDF for printing or Excel for further processing.
Can I control employee permissions?+
Yes. The system uses a precise roles-and-permissions model; you create roles (such as data entry, supervisor, manager) and define for each role what it can view or edit in every module, with an audit log documenting every operation.
What does "multi-year" mean?+
Each fiscal year's data is stored separately, and you can switch between years to view a given year's shipments without mixing them with others — which keeps records organized year after year.
Is my data safe and backed up?+
The system supports sending a data backup by email, in addition to an audit log that records create, update and delete operations — preserving data integrity and the ability to review it.
