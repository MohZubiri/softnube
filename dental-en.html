FAQ

Common Questions About System Features

How do I add a new patient?+
Add a new patient from the Patient section with one click. Enter basic information (name, phone, address), then add medical history and allergies. Data is saved instantly and you can start scheduling appointments.
How do I schedule an appointment?+
Use the interactive calendar to select date and time. The system shows available doctors and rooms. Click your desired appointment and add notes if needed. You can automatically notify the patient.
How do I create multi-payment treatment plans?+
Go to Treatment Plans and create a new plan. Add services and total cost, then set the number of payments and amount per payment. The system tracks payments automatically and sends reminders.
How do I track medical supplies?+
In the Inventory section, add products and set minimum stock levels. You'll receive automatic alerts when stock drops below the threshold. Complete transaction history is maintained.
How do I create professional invoices?+
Go to Invoices, select the patient and services. The system calculates costs automatically. Add discounts or notes, then print or export as PDF. You can also save as draft and update later.
How do I view financial reports?+
The Reports section shows revenue and expenses summaries by period. Filter by doctor, service, or payment type. Print or export as Excel or PDF for review or archiving.
